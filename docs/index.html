G GRECHKA

The diary is the chat. A chat-first iOS calorie tracker — log meals by talking to an assistant.

What it does

You tell GRECHKA what you ate — by typing, scanning a barcode, snapping a photo, or speaking. The assistant extracts every food item, scales kcal and macros to the right serving size, and logs it into your diary. The same chat can recommend what to eat to hit your daily targets, walk you through cooking step-by-step with timers, and answer questions about your day.

Highlights

  • Free / Pro subscription tiers.
  • Local Russian and English support — the assistant replies in your language.
  • Barcode lookup across Open Food Facts, FatSecret, and USDA, with serving-size inferred by the model.
  • Live cooking view with auto-detected step timers.
  • User-created recipes with AI-driven Add-via-chat (Pro).
  • Chat-driven editing of the diary — change weight, remove items, recompute macros.

Status

GRECHKA is currently a working prototype. Builds are produced and installed on physical iPhones; an App Store release is not yet available.
